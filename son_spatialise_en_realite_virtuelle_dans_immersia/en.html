Sound experience in virtual reality in Immersia

We are six students in our fourth year at INSA in computer science.

Immersia : What is it ?

Immersia is a virtual reality platform dedicated to research and more specifically, it is a Cave. A VR Cave is a virtual reality space in the shape of a parallelepiped composed of walls as projection supports creating an immersive experience for users.

NoiseMakers : What is it ?

NoiseMakers is developing audio technologies for post-production and virtual environments. They are reused worldwide to power creative and industrial applications. They run on desktop computers, VR headsets, smartphones, and embedded devices. Here is a brief overview of the company's achievements.

Our objectives :

  • NoiseMakers : To integrate the plugin in Unreal Engine allowing us to use the binaural motor of NoiseMakers to do sound spatialisation in Immersia.

  • Immersia : To develop a demonstrator with Unreal in Immersia to illustrate different sound properties (room effects, localization, moving sounds).

Important sound properties

Binaural

A binaural sound applies filters simulating the differences in time, intensity and timbre between the ears, allowing a more realistic perception of the sound direction.

Ambisonics

An ambisonic sound is a recording and playback technique that captures a three-dimensional sound scene, allowing a 360° immersion.

It encodes the direction and depth of the sound, offering a more realistic spatialization.

Reverberation

Reverberation is the phenomenon of sound reflection on the surfaces of an environment, creating a succession of echoes extending the initial sound.

It allows the perception of space and the distance of a sound source, playing a key role in the sensation of immersion.

Unreal Engine

This is a cutting-edge graphics engine focused on realistic graphics development for video games, simulations, architectural visualizations, and VR. It is proprietary and developed by Epic Games. This engine is free, but takes a share of the revenue generated beyond a certain amount.
